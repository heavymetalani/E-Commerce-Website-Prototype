Boss DS-1X Distortion Guitar Effects Pedal
The DS-1X launches the famous BOSS distortion into a modern era of expression, delivering an unmatched level of performance for guitarists with an ear for tone. This next-generation stomp combines over 35 years of innovation in pedal design with the most advanced technologies, bringing you a completely new tonal experience that’s only possible with the legendary expertise of BOSS. Prepare to be amazed—the inspiring and ultra-responsive DS-1X will change your thinking about distortion pedals forever.

Boss DS-1X Specifications:
o Nominal Input Level: -20 dBu
o Input Impedance: 1 M ohm
o Nominal Output Level: -20 dBu
o Output Impedance: 1 k ohm
o Recommended Load Impedance: 10 k ohms or greater
o Controls: Pedal switch, LEVEL knob, LOW knob, HIGH knob, DIST knob
o Indicator: CHECK indicator (Serves also as battery check indicator)
o Connectors: INPUT jack, OUTPUT jack, AC adaptor jack (DC 9 V)
o Power Supply: Alkaline battery (9V, 6LR61) or Carbon-zinc battery (9V, 6F22), AC adaptor (not included)
o Current Draw: 45 mA.
