Cort X Custom
The new X-Custom is equipped with a double locking II tremolo for tuning stability and smooth tremolo action. The Seymour Duncan SH1('59) & SH4(JB) pickups(H-H) on the X-Custom provide premium humbucker punch and warmth.

Cort X Custom Specifications:
o Construction: Bolt-On
o Body: Basswood
o Neck: 3pc Canadian Hard Maple
o Binding: N/A
o Fretboard: Rosewood, 15 3/4" Radius(400mm)
o Frets: 24 / Jumbo (2.9mm)
o Scale: 25 1/2" (648mm)
o Inlay: White Pearl Sharp Triangle
o Tuners: Die Cast
o Bridge: Double Locking Ii Tremolo
o Pickups: Seymour Duncan Sh1('59) & Sh4(Jb) (H-H)
o Electronics: 1 Volume, 1 Tone W/ Push Pull, 3way Lever
o Hardware: Bn (Black Nickel)
o Strings: D'addario Exl120(042-009)
o Colors: BKM - Black Metallic.
