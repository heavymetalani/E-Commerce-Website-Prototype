Roland Micro Cube GX Amplifier
Chosen Best of Show at Summer NAMM 2013! The Roland Micro Cube GX brings new functionality and power to the most popular portable amp combo on the market. Weighing in at just six pounds, this compact guitar amplifier packs a big punch and comes with eight great DSP effects, eight COSM amp models, and a built-in chromatic tuner, giving you killer tones that you can take anywhere! Fully embracing the digital age, the Micro Cube GX features Roland's i-Cube Link input, which lets you connect your favorite iOS device to play music or interface with apps. The Roland Micro Cube GX is a complete practice, recording, and performance amp in a portable package.

Roland Micro Cube GX Specifications:
o Type: Solid State
o Number of Channels: 1
o Power: 3W
o Speakers: 1 x 5"
o Effects: Yes
o Reverb: Yes
o EQ: Tone
o Amp Modeling: Yes
o Number of Models: 7
o Preamp Tubes: No Tubes
o Power Tubes: No Tubes
o Inputs: 1 x Instrument, 1 x Aux (1/8")
o Outputs: 1 x Rec Out/Headphones (1/8")
o Footswitch I/O: No Footswitch
o Effects Loop: No Effects Loop.
